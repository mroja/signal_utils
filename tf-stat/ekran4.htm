Screen 4:

In this step you set parameters that control the axes

Parameters:

  • time – sets the range of time axis 

  • frequency – sets the range of frequency axis  

  • reference period – time range of reference period where the baseline level is computed  

  • right margin – margin of the map that can be distorted due tu border effects 

  • frequency tick – number of Hz between frequency ticks  

  • time tick – number of seconds between time ticks  

  • lines – time positions of marker lines
