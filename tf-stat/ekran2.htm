Screen 2:

In this step you configure parameters related to the signal

Parameters:

  • samples – number of signal samples in a single trial 

  • sampling – sampling frequency 

  • Number of trials – number of the experiment repetitions

  • option valid only for CWT 

    • Morlet wavelet par. – Morlet wavelet constant  

  • Options valid only for MP

    • Min. periods in waveform – Minimal number of periods of MP time-frequency atoms used in the construction of t-f energy distribution maps  

    • Accepted scales – range of lengths of MP time-frequency atoms used in the construction of t-f energy distribution maps
